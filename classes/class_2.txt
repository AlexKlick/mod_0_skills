Class Drinks

Attributes:

capacity_ml (integer)
alcohol_content (float)
current_amount_ml (integer)
has_ice (boolean)

Method:

fill_glass  (takes capacity_ml - current_amount and adds that amount to the glass)
add_shot (adds 1 30 mL shot to the beverage increasing alcohol_content proportionally)
add_ice (changes has_ice from false to true, icreases current_amount_ml by 50)
