Class Chef

Attributes:
name (string)
years_experience (integer)
cooking_proficiency ('string')
signature_dish ('string')

Methods:

boil_water (the chef is preparing his signature dish, Shrimp-and-Lobster Ravioli and
needs to boil water for the Ravioli)
check_cue (the chef checks what menu items have been ordered, if it is a particular chefs
signature_dish, then he or she will be the one to make it)
prepare_pastries (the chef that has cooking_proficiency set as 'pastry_chef' will prepare delicious desserts)
check_dishes_before_serving (the lead chef with the greatest years_experience will check over the other chef's work
and make sure everything is up to par)
